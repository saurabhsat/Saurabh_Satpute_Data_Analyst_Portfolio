Revenue Insights in Hospitality Domain

This project, "Revenue Insights in the Hospitality Domain," focuses on analyzing revenue performance in the hotel industry using a dataset that includes booking details, room information, and hotel properties. By examining patterns in customer bookings, room categories, and booking statuses, the analysis aims to provide insights into revenue generation and realization across different hotel classes and booking platforms. The project also explores customer behavior by evaluating booking trends, occupancy rates, and ratings, contributing to a data-driven understanding of factors that influence revenue in the hospitality sector.
